Getting Started

GoDark Video AI transforms your text prompts into highly detailed, cinematic videos using Replicate's state-of-the-art models.

  • Navigate to Create Video to start.
  • Write a detailed, descriptive narrative prompt.
  • Select your preferred visual style, duration, and aspect ratio.
  • Click Generate and wait for the secure serverless backend to poll Replicate and return your video.

Data Storage

All videos are saved locally on your device using IndexedDB. Export your favorites to keep them permanently!
