Getting Started

GoDark Video AI transforms your text prompts into highly detailed, cinematic videos using the state-of-the-art cerspense/zeroscope_v2_576w model.

  • Go to the Profile (top right) to enter your Hugging Face API Token.
  • Navigate to Create Video to start.
  • Write a detailed, descriptive narrative prompt.
  • Select your preferred visual style, duration, and aspect ratio.
  • Click Generate and wait for the model to synthesize your video.

Tips for Better Prompts

To get the most cinematic results, use rich descriptive keywords such as:

  • Lighting: "cinematic lighting, volumetric fog, neon glow"
  • Camera: "drone shot, panning, slow motion, 8k resolution"
  • Environment: "cyberpunk city, hyper-realistic, dark atmosphere"

Data Storage

All videos are saved locally on your device using IndexedDB. No videos are stored on our servers. Export your favorites to keep them permanently!
